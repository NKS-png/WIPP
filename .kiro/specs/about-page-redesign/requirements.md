# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The WIPP About page redesign aims to create a calm, honest, and artist-friendly introduction to the platform that builds trust and clearly communicates WIPP's unique value proposition. The page must feel authentic to artists while avoiding corporate marketing language and startup buzzwords.

## Glossary

- **WIPP**: Work In Progress - a community-first platform for artists focused on unfinished work and structured feedback
- **About_Page**: The primary informational page that introduces visitors to WIPP's purpose and community
- **Content_Management_System**: The system that manages and displays the About page content
- **Artist_Community**: The target audience of creative professionals seeking feedback and growth
- **Feedback_Structure**: The opt-in, structured critique system that differentiates WIPP from other platforms

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Emotional Connection and Opening

**User Story:** As a visiting artist, I want to immediately feel understood and see my creative struggles reflected, so that I know this platform was built for people like me.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor loads the About page, THE About_Page SHALL display a quiet opening section that addresses common artist experiences with unfinished work
2. WHEN the opening content is displayed, THE About_Page SHALL use relatable language about creative doubt and growth without being overly dramatic
3. THE About_Page SHALL present the opening in short paragraphs with ample white space for calm readability
4. WHEN an artist reads the opening, THE About_Page SHALL create an emotional connection without using corporate marketing language

### Requirement 2: Clear Platform Definition

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want to understand what WIPP is in plain language, so that I can quickly determine if it's relevant to my needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor seeks platform information, THE About_Page SHALL explain WIPP as a place for artists focused on process over perfection
2. THE About_Page SHALL clearly state that WIPP operates without algorithm pressure or social media mechanics
3. WHEN describing the platform, THE About_Page SHALL use accessible language that any artist can understand
4. THE About_Page SHALL avoid startup buzzwords and growth-focused terminology

### Requirement 3: Problem Context and Differentiation

**User Story:** As an artist frustrated with existing platforms, I want to understand why WIPP exists and what problems it solves, so that I can see how it addresses my specific pain points.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN explaining WIPP's purpose, THE About_Page SHALL articulate problems with public platforms that reward polish over progress
2. THE About_Page SHALL explain why trust and structured feedback matter more than reach and engagement
3. WHEN describing platform differentiation, THE About_Page SHALL emphasize opt-in critique as skilled labor
4. THE About_Page SHALL validate artists' reluctance to share unfinished work publicly

### Requirement 4: Functional Overview

**User Story:** As someone considering joining WIPP, I want to understand how the platform works at a high level, so that I can envision using it effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN describing functionality, THE About_Page SHALL explain the portfolio space for finished work
2. THE About_Page SHALL describe the workshop space for structured feedback
3. WHEN outlining features, THE About_Page SHALL mention small community formation capabilities
4. THE About_Page SHALL present functional information without overwhelming technical details

### Requirement 5: Clear Boundaries and Expectations

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want to understand what WIPP is NOT, so that I have realistic expectations and know if it aligns with my goals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE About_Page SHALL explicitly state that WIPP is not social media
2. THE About_Page SHALL clarify that WIPP is not a popularity contest or marketplace
3. WHEN setting expectations, THE About_Page SHALL emphasize that WIPP prioritizes growth over viral reach
4. THE About_Page SHALL help visitors self-select based on their values and goals

### Requirement 6: Target Audience Identification

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to know if WIPP is designed for artists like me, so that I can decide whether to invest time in exploring the platform.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN describing the target audience, THE About_Page SHALL identify artists who want to improve their craft
2. THE About_Page SHALL appeal to artists who value constructive feedback over likes and shares
3. WHEN addressing audience fit, THE About_Page SHALL acknowledge that WIPP suits artists comfortable with slow, meaningful growth
4. THE About_Page SHALL help artists self-identify as good fits for the community

### Requirement 7: Authentic Closing and Invitation

**User Story:** As someone who has read the About page, I want to feel invited to explore further without pressure, so that I can make an authentic decision about joining.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN concluding the page, THE About_Page SHALL provide an open-ended, non-demanding invitation
2. THE About_Page SHALL acknowledge that WIPP itself is a work in progress
3. WHEN closing, THE About_Page SHALL avoid sales-focused calls to action
4. THE About_Page SHALL maintain the thoughtful, artist-friendly tone throughout the conclusion

### Requirement 8: Visual and Stylistic Consistency

**User Story:** As a reader, I want the About page to feel calm and thoughtful throughout, so that the design supports the content's message about WIPP being different.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE About_Page SHALL use short paragraphs with generous white space throughout
2. THE About_Page SHALL maintain a consistent tone that sounds like a thoughtful artist, not a founder
3. WHEN presenting content, THE About_Page SHALL avoid emojis and corporate design elements
4. THE About_Page SHALL ensure visual design supports the calm, honest messaging

### Requirement 9: Content Management and Updates

**User Story:** As a WIPP administrator, I want to be able to update About page content easily, so that the page can evolve as the platform grows.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN content needs updating, THE Content_Management_System SHALL allow easy editing of all About page sections
2. THE Content_Management_System SHALL maintain the structured format while allowing content flexibility
3. WHEN changes are made, THE Content_Management_System SHALL preserve the page's visual consistency
4. THE Content_Management_System SHALL support version control for content changes
